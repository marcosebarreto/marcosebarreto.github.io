I am an Assistant Professorial Lecturer in the Department of Statistics at the London School of Economics and Political Science. I teach Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning and Distributed Computing for Big Data, and Databases at postgraduate and graduate levels. I am also the Head Class Teacher for the Programming for Data Science course in the BSc Data Science and Business Analytics online programme offered by University of London.

I am an Affiliate with the LSE Data Science Institute, involved in teaching and research activities around data science. I am also an Associate Researcher at CIDACS (FIOCRUZ Bahia), a data linkage and analytics centre in northeast Brazil conducting epidemiological studies over massive population-based cohorts comprising socioeconomic and healthcare governmental data; and at the CPA-IA - Centre of Applied Research on Artificial Intelligence for Industry (SENAI-CIMATEC Bahia, Brazil).
