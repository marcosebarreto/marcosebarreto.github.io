My whereabouts

  • I was born in Curitiba, Brazil.
  • In 1983, I moved to São Borja, in the western boarder of Rio Grande do Sul state with Argentina.
  • In 1991, I moved to Porto Alegre, capital city of Rio Grande do Sul, and lived there and nearby cities for 19 years.
  • In 2000, during my Master, I did a five-month research internship in Grenoble, France, at the Laboratoire d'Informatique de Grenoble.
  • From August to December 2002, I was a visiting professor at Universitat Ramon Llull, in Barcelona, Spain.
  • From 2010 to 2020, I lived in Salvador, capital city of Bahia and work at the Federal University of Bahia (UFBA).
  • In November 2016, I moved to London for a postdoctoral research at the Institute of Health Informatics (University College London).
  • From March 2018 to November 2020, I was back at my job at UFBA, in Salvador, Brazil.
  • Since December 2020, I am in London working at the London School of Economics and Political Science.
  • So far, I have traveled to different cities and countries: 15 (out of 27) federation units in Brazil, Argentina, México, Portugal, Spain, Italy, France, Belgium, The Netherlands, Austria, Hungary, and Vietnam (still a long way ahead :))
